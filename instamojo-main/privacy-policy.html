About US

Womela was established on june 12 2015, at Banglore. Owned by Chandini Bala, a international certified cosmatologist, Bridal makeup artist & Trainee

At 2015, womea named as womea makeover, started as freelancer did only bridal makeup later, at 2019 owned by bridal studio at Erode and named as womea makeup, fashion institute & cosmetology clinic cum bridal makeup studio

The main motive of womea is to provide education at minimal cost who everyone can able to purchase this dream course mainly home makers & working women can purchase their loved course at their flexible time online even offline also.

Proudly, At 2020 womea certified us ISO 9001-2015 academy and also at 2023, Womea affliated by Central Government as we started to give government certification to students

We translate our vision into real output by nurturing the natural creativity of our students with proven creative techniques and by enhancing their creative productivity using smart software. This twin-fold pedagogical strategy makes our students both creative and smart. In addition, we also ensure that the students gain technical and commercial knowledge that the profession demands so that they become readily employable and successful entrepreneurs.
