Course Description

Aari design is a traditional Indian embroidery technique that uses a hooked needle, known as an Aari needle, to create intricate designs on fabrics. Aari design is a popular form of embroidery that is used to embellish clothing, home décor items, and accessories.

If you are interested in learning Aari design, there are several courses available online and offline. These courses cover the basics of Aari embroidery, including the types of stitches used, the materials and tools required, and the techniques for creating various designs.

What you'll learn
  • Introduction to Aari embroidery and its history
  • Types of fabrics and threads used in Aari embroidery
  • Understanding the Aari needle and its usage
  • Various types of stitches used in Aari embroidery
  • Embellishing clothing, home decor items, and accessories with Aari embroidery
  • Troubleshooting common issues faced during Aari embroidery
  • Maintaining Aari embroidery tools and materials
Requirements
  • You will need a copy of Adobe XD 2019 or above. A free trial can be downloaded from Adobe.
  • No previous design experience is needed.
  • No previous Adobe XD skills are needed.
